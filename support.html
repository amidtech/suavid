FAQ


Suavid is being actively developed and maintained. More features will be added soon. Most of the current features are way straightforward on its own.

If you could not find your answer here, feel free to contact us,
support@suavid.com

#1 How to record a movie?

Suavid got a quick and modern way to do this.
Press and hold the record button to start recording. Lift up your finger off the button to pause.

Press and hold again to resume recording as is. You can repeat this cycle several times, record here and there.

To stop recording, just hit the red square button by the side of the record button, which only shows up as recording pauses.

#2 How do I continue recording without holding the record button?

Press and hold the record button, and move your finger toward the center of the screen until you see a label which says “release to continue”. Then you may release your finger, and it will continue to record without pause.

#3 May I pause the recording and lock the screen or do something else like launch another app?

YES. As long as you do not launch another video recording app, you can do whatever you want. And when you come back, you may resume recording as usual.

However, some apps consume much memory, and they may kill apps that are running in the background. Then you couldn’t resume your recording when you are back to Suavid. Don’t worry about your last recording. It has already been saved properly. You won’t lost anything.

!CAUTION: If you start recording a video with another app while Suavid is still in a pause state, it may impair your current recording in Suavid.

#4 Am I be able to record a silent movie, or mute the sound for a few seconds and turn on the sound again?

Sure. You may toggle between mute on and off anytime by touching that microphone button at the upper left corner(suppose you are hold your device in a portrait mode).

#5 Can I delete a batch of movies and photos at a time?

YES. Go to the gallery, long press any item and it will enter edit mode. Select photos and movies that you want to delete, then hit the trash icon to remove those files.
